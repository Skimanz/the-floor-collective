What to expect

What you can expect

Flooring looks straightforward until something goes wrong. A floor installed without proper subfloor preparation, correct expansion gaps, or the right finish for the timber species will show problems within a year or two.

We focus on flooring — installation, sanding, staircase and decking for residential and commercial properties across Queenstown.

  • Correct subfloor assessment and preparation before any board goes down
  • Species-appropriate finish selection for long-term performance
  • Proper acclimatisation of timber before installation
  • Expansion gap management for Queenstown's climate variation
  • Dustless sanding that protects your home and family
