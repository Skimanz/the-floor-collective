About us

About The Flooring Collective

"Local and straightforward."

The Flooring Collective is a Queenstown-based flooring business offering engineered timber installation, floor sanding and restoration, and outdoor decking for residential and commercial properties.

Our story

Why we started The Flooring Collective

We started The Flooring Collective because Queenstown deserved a flooring team that does one thing well, rather than a dozen things at once. Every job, big or small, gets the same level of care from start to finish.

What to expect

What you can expect

  • Subfloor preparation

    We check and prepare the subfloor before installation begins, so the finished floor sits flat and true.

  • Finish selection

    We talk through oil, polyurethane and hardwax options so you choose the right finish for your floor.

  • Acclimatisation

    Timber is left to acclimatise to your home's conditions before installation, reducing movement after laying.

  • Expansion gaps

    Correct expansion gaps are left at the perimeter so the floor can move naturally with the seasons.

  • Dustless sanding

    Sanding is carried out with dust extraction equipment to keep your home clean during the process.
