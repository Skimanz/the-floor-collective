What to expect

What you can expect

  • Subfloor preparation

    We check and prepare the subfloor before installation begins, so the finished floor sits flat and true.

  • Finish selection

    We talk through oil, polyurethane and hardwax options so you choose the right finish for your floor.

  • Acclimatisation

    Timber is left to acclimatise to your home's conditions before installation, reducing movement after laying.

  • Expansion gaps

    Correct expansion gaps are left at the perimeter so the floor can move naturally with the seasons.

  • Dustless sanding

    Sanding is carried out with dust extraction equipment to keep your home clean during the process.
