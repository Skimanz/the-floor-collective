What we do

Flooring Services

Indoor and outdoor flooring services across Queenstown for residential and commercial properties.

Indoor Flooring

Service 01

Engineered Timber Installation

Installation of engineered timber floors. Subfloor preparation included — we ensure the base is right before a single board goes down.

Includes

  • Engineered timber floor installation
  • Subfloor preparation and levelling
  • Glue-down and floating methods
  • Expansion gap and skirting finishing

Service 02

Sanding & Restoration

Dustless sanding and refinishing for tired or damaged timber floors. We bring the original character back without the mess.

Includes

  • Dustless sanding equipment
  • Stain and finish matching
  • Gap filling and board repairs
  • Oil, polyurethane or hardwax finishes

Service 03

Laminate Flooring

A durable, cost-effective alternative to solid timber. Laminate flooring supplied and installed for residential and commercial spaces.

Includes

  • Laminate floor installation
  • Underlay and moisture barrier
  • Skirting and trim finishing
  • Wide range of finishes available

Service 04

Floor Repairs

Damaged boards, squeaky floors, water damage — we assess and repair timber and laminate floors to get them back in shape.

Includes

  • Board replacement and patching
  • Squeak and movement fixes
  • Water damage assessment and repair
  • Colour and finish matching

Outdoor Flooring

Service 05

Timber Decking

Outdoor decking and deck framing built to handle Queenstown's seasons — from alpine winters to dry summers.

Includes

  • Deck framing and substructure
  • Timber decking board installation
  • Stairs and balustrade options
  • Weather-rated finishing
