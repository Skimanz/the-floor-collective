What we do

Services built around your floor

We focus exclusively on floor installation and finishing — specialist knowledge applied to every job we take on.

Service 01

Timber Floor Installation

Solid hardwood and engineered timber floors installed to the highest standard. We prepare the subfloor properly before a single board goes down — because a great floor starts beneath the surface.

We work with your chosen supplier or can recommend trusted local timber yards in Queenstown.

Includes

  • Solid hardwood installation
  • Engineered timber installation
  • Subfloor preparation and levelling
  • Moisture barrier installation
  • Glue-down and floating systems
  • Trim and threshold finishing

Service 03

Sanding & Finishing

Dustless sanding equipment means minimal disruption to your home and family. Once sanded, we help you choose the right finish for your floor and your lifestyle.

Finish options

  • Polyurethane — durable, low maintenance, gloss to matte
  • Hardwax oil — natural look, easy to spot-repair
  • Penetrating oil — enhances natural grain, matte finish
  • Re-coating of existing finishes

Repairs & Restoration

Worn, damaged, or tired floors don't always need replacing. We assess what's there and restore it — board replacement, gap filling, re-coating, and colour matching to make repairs seamless.

  • Gap filling and crack repair
  • Re-coating and refinishing
  • Water damage assessment and repair
  • Squeaky board rectification

Floor finishes

The right finish makes the floor

Polyurethane

The most durable finish available. Hard-wearing, water-resistant, and easy to maintain. Available in gloss, satin, and matte sheens.

Most durable

Hardwax Oil

A natural, penetrating oil and wax blend that enhances the grain and warmth of timber. Spot-repairable without sanding the whole floor.

Natural look

Penetrating Oil

Soaks into the timber rather than sitting on top. Creates a matte, close-to-natural finish that breathes with the wood.

Matte finish
