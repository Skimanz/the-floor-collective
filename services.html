What we do

Services built around your floor

Indoor or outdoor — we focus exclusively on floor and deck installation and finishing. Specialist knowledge applied to every job.

Timber Floor Installation

Solid hardwood and engineered timber floors installed to the highest standard. We prepare the subfloor properly before a single board goes down — because a great floor starts beneath the surface.

We work with your chosen supplier or can recommend trusted local timber yards in Queenstown.

Includes

  • Solid hardwood installation
  • Engineered timber installation
  • Subfloor preparation and levelling
  • Moisture barrier installation
  • Glue-down and floating systems
  • Trim and threshold finishing

Service 06

Nail-Free Timber Decking

We install the ShadowDeck™ nail-free timber decking system — a premium hidden fastener solution that creates a seamless, screw-free surface. No visible nails or screws. Barefoot friendly. Built to last outdoors.

Pre-grooved Kwila hardwood boards clip into hidden fasteners for a clean, professional finish that allows the timber to naturally expand and contract with the seasons.

Includes

  • ShadowDeck™ system installation
  • Pre-grooved Kwila timber boards
  • Hidden fastener fixing
  • Picture framing and edge finishing
  • Staircase tread installation

Service 07

Aluminium Deck Framing

We install the SuperSpan™ aluminium subframing system — the professional's choice for outdoor decking. Lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and PS1 certified by EQ Struc for building consent applications.

The clever design lets you switch between timber decking and tiles without changing your framing. Height-adjustable Nurajack pedestals make installation fast, precise, and up to 80% faster than traditional timber framing.

Features

  • SuperSpan™ aluminium joist and bearer installation
  • PS1 certified — suitable for building consent
  • Nurajack pedestal installation and levelling
  • Compatible with timber decking and tiles
  • 30mm, 60mm and 120mm profile options
  • Low-clearance builds from 30mm
