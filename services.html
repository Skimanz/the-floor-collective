What we do

Flooring Services

Indoor and outdoor flooring services across Queenstown for residential and commercial properties.

Indoor Flooring

Service 01

Engineered Timber Installation

Installation of engineered timber floors. Subfloor preparation included — we ensure the base is right before a single board goes down.

Includes

  • Engineered timber floor installation
  • Subfloor preparation and levelling
  • Glue-down and floating methods
  • Expansion gap and skirting finishing

Service 02

Sanding & Finishing

Sanding and finishing. Oil, polyurethane, or hardwax — we help you choose the right finish for your floor and lifestyle.

Includes

  • Full floor sanding
  • Stain application (optional)
  • Oil, polyurethane or hardwax finish
  • Edge and corner sanding

Service 03

Staircase Flooring

Stair treads, risers, and nosings in matching or complementary timber to complete the look from floor to floor.

Includes

  • Tread and riser cladding
  • Nosing installation
  • Timber matching to existing floors
  • New build and retrofit

Outdoor Flooring

Service 04

Timber Decking

Installation of nail-free timber decking — hidden fasteners create a seamless, screw-free surface. No visible nails or screws. Barefoot friendly.

Pre-grooved Kwila hardwood boards clip into hidden fasteners for a clean finish that allows the timber to naturally expand and contract with the seasons.

Includes

  • Nail-free decking system installation
  • Pre-grooved Kwila timber boards
  • Hidden fastener fixing
  • Picture framing and edge finishing
  • Staircase tread installation

Service 05

Aluminium Deck Framing

Installation of aluminium subframing system — lightweight, durable, and PS1 certified. Compatible with both timber decking and tiles, with height-adjustable Nurajack pedestals for a fast, precise finish.

Includes

  • Aluminium joist and bearer installation
  • PS1 certified — suitable for building consent
  • Nurajack pedestal installation and levelling
  • Compatible with timber decking and tiles
  • 30mm, 60mm and 120mm profile options

Floor finishes

Choosing your finish

Polyurethane

A hard-wearing surface coat that sits on top of the timber. Durable and easy to clean — ideal for high-traffic areas.

Most durable

Hardwax Oil

A natural, penetrating oil and wax blend that enhances the grain and warmth of timber. Spot-repairable without sanding the whole floor.

Natural look

Penetrating Oil

Soaks into the timber rather than sitting on top. Creates a matte, close-to-natural finish that breathes with the wood.

Matte finish
