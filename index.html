Floor Installation and Restoration

The Flooring Collective provides quality flooring solutions throughout Queenstown, offering engineered timber, hardwood, hybrid, laminate and luxury vinyl plank flooring, floor sanding and restoration, plus timber and composite decking with aluminium deck framing for residential and commercial projects.

About us

We offer

Flooring Services

Indoor and outdoor flooring services across Queenstown for residential and commercial properties.

Installation

Installation of engineered timber floors. Subfloor preparation included — we ensure the base is right before a single board goes down.

Sanding & Finishing

Sanding and finishing. Oil, polyurethane, or hardwax — we help you choose the right finish for your floor and lifestyle.

Staircase

Stair treads, risers, and nosings in matching or complementary timber to complete the look from floor to floor.

Timber Decking

Installation of nail-free timber decking system — hidden fasteners create a seamless, screw-free surface that is barefoot friendly.

Aluminium Deck Framing

Installation of aluminium subframing system — lightweight, durable, and PS1 certified. Compatible with both timber decking and tiles.
